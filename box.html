BOX

Kinetic sculpture

2014


One of the second - semester - challenges was to create and to stage movement framed in a box out of wood. The students were free to choose which kind of scene they wanna create with the only limitation, that it should happen inside the box in a closed systhem, so I started thinking about movement, dramaturgy and telling a story just by little motion. The result is an abstract wire - sculpture that morphs into a portrait throuth rotation. Just through the movement the picture becomes viewable.
