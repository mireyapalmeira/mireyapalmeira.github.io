Antischall Ohrdeckel / ANC ear caps

Speculative design

2016


»Cocktail party effect« describes the human ability to extract a single sound source out of many in a noisy environment, to focus on this one and perceive everything else less strong. The »Antischall-Ohrdeckel« translate this phenomenon into the digital space. They are a tool for the individual regulation and composition of the sourrounding soundscape. They work with a combination of different technologies including sound localization, intelligent databases and active noise control (sound waves which are artificially made to wipe out other sound waves through destructive interference). In a world that is shaped by a new class of sounds that came with the industrial revolution, the »Antischall-Ohrdeckel« enable the selective hearing, they allow the user to sense and discover nature through the auditory perception.
