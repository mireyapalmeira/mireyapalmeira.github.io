Rundgang

Campaign theme

2015


The »Rundgang« is the biggest University event of the year, where all students exhibit their works. It comes with a big publicity campaign that contains posters all over the city, digital advertisement, a commercial for the website of the University and the »Berliner Fenster« (the public transportation television), an detailed article at the »Tagesspiegel« (the cities daily newspaper), and other print media like flyers and a brochure. To find the campaign theme there is a university internal competition every year. A jury chooses one theme out of all designed posters.
For the Rundgang in summer 2015 my theme was choosen.
